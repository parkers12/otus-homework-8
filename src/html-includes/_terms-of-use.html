Terms of use

Terms of use of the site is a document that is a road map for users, it regulates the relationship between the user and the owner of the project / copyright holder.

In the Terms of use (User agreement), it is important to settle at least the following legal relationships:

  • conditions and procedure for using the site;
  • the procedure for creating an account, as well as deleting it (including at the initiative of the project);
  • detailed description of services / rules of sale and purchase and return of goods;
  • a responsibility;
  • intellectual property issues;
  • the procedure for collecting personal data of users and working with them (but in fact this is another large layer of legal issues, therefore best practice is a separate document "privacy policy" about the presence and obligation of which should still be indicated in the rules of use);
  • ist of actions that are prohibited;
  • In the User Agreement of the site, it is necessary to describe in detail what functions are available that can be accessed without registration and what additional functionality of the site is available to users after registration.

Legal documents for certain categories of sites, such as blogs, may be considered unnecessary, but in some cases they are required and in all cases are very important for both you and the site visitors.

The last two points are especially relevant for sites where users can post content. There is always a risk of receiving claims for violation of intellectual property rights of third parties or for the protection of honor, dignity and business reputation for disseminating information that is negative, false and subject to refutation. Indeed, in many jurisdictions, when considering cases where it is impossible to identify the defendant (author), or the information was posted anonymously, the owner of the site will be responsible.
